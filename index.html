Get-Well Soup

Cook Time: 10 minutes
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes

A quick and healthy soup that's perfect to help you feel better when you're sick.

Cuisine: Asian

Servings: 2

Recipe by: Valerie Hare

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 onion sliced in wedges
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 4 cage-free eggs
  • Dash of salt and pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup of spinach (optional)
  • Sliced green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Saute sliced onions in a medium-size pot over medium-high heat for a few minutes or until translucent.
  2. Reduce to medium heat. Add minced garlic to the pot and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken broth, salt, pepper, turmeric, cayenne, and spinach (optional) to the pot. Bring to a soft boil over high-heat.
  4. Rinse eggs and crack them individually into the pot carefully.
  5. Reduce to medium-low heat, stirring the soup occasionally for a couple minutes.
  6. Remove the pot from heat.
  7. Enjoy your delicious, warm soup. Garnish with sliced green onions for a pop of color and flavor.
